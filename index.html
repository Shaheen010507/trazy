About us

In the heartbeat of our college, between laughter, lectures, and late-night assignments… there’s one thing we all crave — good food, served without the wait.
Trazy was born not from a lab or a boardroom, but from a hungry thought whispered across a crowded canteen:
“Why do we always have to wait so long?”
And so, we built something beautiful.
A little spark of tech magic, right here on campus.
A friend that lets you order your favorite meals — quietly, quickly, with just a tap.
No queues. No pushing. Just peace, and plates of comfort.


🌟 Made With Care

Trazy isn’t just an app. It’s a promise — that your break time matters, that your chai shouldn’t come with chaos, and that even in a buzzing campus, your order can find its way to you… fresh, warm, and waiting.

🕊️ So next time you’re hungry... Don’t rush. Don’t wait. Just Trazy. Because good food should never come with stress — only joy.
